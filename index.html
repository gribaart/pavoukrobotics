FAQ

Questions?

When does it ship?
First batch ships 2026. Orders are fulfilled in the order received. You get a confirmation email with your queue number right after ordering.
Do I need tools or soldering?
None at all. Every connection uses simple snap clips. No tools, no soldering. Assembly takes 1 to 2 hours with our illustrated picture guide.
What is the AI subscription?
The AI Brain subscription unlocks voice assistant, autonomous behavior, and long-term memory. Starter includes 3 months free, Pro includes 1 year, Lifetime includes 3 years. After that it is €20 per month or €199 per year. Pavouk is fully playable without any subscription.
What app do I need?
Nothing to download. The Pavouk app runs in any modern phone browser. Open it, connect to your robot, and play. Works on iPhone and Android.
What is your return policy?
30-day money back guarantee, no questions asked. If Pavouk arrives damaged or defective we replace it immediately at no cost.
What age is it for?
Pavouk is designed for ages 8 and up. The Lifetime kit arrives fully assembled — perfect for younger children aged 6+. The Starter kit is ideal for ages 10+ or as a parent-child project.
Do you ship across the EU?
Yes, we ship to all EU countries. For countries outside the EU please write to hello@pavouk.robot.
